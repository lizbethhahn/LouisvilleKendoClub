About Louisville Kendo

Louisville Kendo Club is the local Kendo club for the greater Louisville area. Founded in 2004, Louisville Kendo Club is a non-profit organization dedicated to the practice and teaching of kendo and is affiliated with the East Central U.S. Kendo Federation (ECUSKF) and is part of the All United States Kendo Federation (AUSKF).

The concept and purpose of practicing kendo, according the All Japan Kendo Federation:
